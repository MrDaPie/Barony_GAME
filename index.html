Game Features

  • Assemble your fantasy adventure party for a dungeon crawl! With a class for every playstyle, each player offers their own skills and personality to the quest.

  • Adaptability and resourcefulness are key! Whether adventuring solo or with friends, take full advantage of your spells, potions, weapons and skills to emerge victorious against the gauntlet of monsters and traps.

  • No two adventures are ever alike, and few survive to taste victory. Chase hundreds of achievements and experiment with unique party makeups for unlimited replayability.

  • Seamless split-screen for 1-4 players, without compromise.

  • Play together regardless of where you bought the game. Windows, Mac & PC players can play with one another using Direct-IP or Crossplay. Nintendo Switch players can join in with Crossplay too!

  • Play on-the-go with friends using the Nintendo Switch Wireless Play feature. Pick up and play whenever you have a few spare minutes to run through a dungeon floor or two.

  • Meticulously crafted voxel and pixel art, capturing the illustrated style of VGA PC-RPGs of the early 90s. Inspired by the masterful artwork of Ultima Underworld, Daggerfall and Lands of Lore: The Throne of Chaos.

Story

Hamlet is the greatest little city in the realm. That is, it was, until the heinous Baron Herx came along and claimed the whole thing as his Barony. He enslaved Hamlet's people and now he's an awful undead lich. The whole town's been trapped in his massive dungeon for decades and now it's up to a single hero or a group of adventurers to confront him in his hellish lair and destroy him forever. Whether you will die in the dungeons or rise to victory against the Baron and his hellspawn is ultimately up to you.
